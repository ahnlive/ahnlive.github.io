Music

"You Are"

A Melodic House track inspired by the likes of the This Never Happened (TNH) music label. I spent about a week on this project as a novice using FL Studio. The main plugins used were LABS Soft Piano, Vital, and Flux Mini 2 for sidechaining, along with a free sample/preset pack I found online. I used the trial version of FL Studio, meaning I could not close the program throughout my production (I would have to pay to open it again).

Over the course of a day, I learned how to use Unreal Engine 5 to produce the visualizer in the video. The entire process costed $0.
