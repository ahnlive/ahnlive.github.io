about me

My name is Ayden, a senior student at Burnaby North High School aspiring to pursue a career in aerospace. From a young age I fostered an interest in technology and would take apart electronic devices to see how they functioned. Humble beginnings led to several projects I am passionate about, including an Arduino-controlled RC aircraft, a personal server, a human-powered phone charger, and an open-source AdBlock service. I am committed to bringing a positive impact to the world through technology and engineering.
